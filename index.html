Facial Recognition in the News

the top terms used to talk about facial recognition in english language news sources in 2019

By: Rahul Bhargava

The Density Design lab does wonderful work visualizing nuanced stories. A recent project in their Final Synthesis Design Studio course focused on facial recognition. Thier second research question was particularly interesting to me: "In which terms are people talking about facial recognition on the most popular social media platforms in U.S. (and other English speaking countries), China, Italy and India?" Inspired by their 2nd research question and visualization, I decided to look at how facial recognition was covered in English-language online news in various countries.

Initial Observations

Facebook is the top related term in all of these countries.
